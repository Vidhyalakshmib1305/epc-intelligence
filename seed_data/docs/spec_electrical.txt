ELECTRICAL SPECIFICATION - DATA CENTRE BLOCK A
Project: Hyperscale Data Centre - Chennai Campus
Document No: ELEC-SPEC-001 Rev 2
Issued: January 2026

1. SCOPE
This specification covers the design, supply, installation, testing and commissioning 
of all electrical systems for Data Centre Block A including HV/LV distribution, 
UPS systems, standby generation, and busbar systems.

2. UPS SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
2.1 Topology: Double conversion online UPS, minimum N+1 redundancy
2.2 Capacity: 2N configuration, 2 x 1000 kVA per module row
2.3 Efficiency: Minimum 96% at full load, 98% in ECO mode
2.4 Battery backup: Minimum 15 minutes at full load
2.5 Input voltage: 415V +/-10%, 3 phase, 50 Hz
2.6 THDI: Less than 3% with active front end
2.7 Accepted makes: Eaton, Schneider Electric, Vertiv, ABB

3. GENERATOR REQUIREMENTS  
3.1 Capacity: 3 x 2500 kVA diesel generators (N+1)
3.2 Fuel tank: Minimum 24 hours at full load
3.3 Start time: Generator online within 10 seconds of mains failure
3.4 Synchronisation: Automatic synchronisation panel required
3.5 Emissions: Compliant with CPCB norms, Tier 4 equivalent
3.6 Accepted makes: Cummins, Caterpillar, Perkins, Kohler

4. HV DISTRIBUTION
4.1 Incoming: 33kV from TNEB, dual feed from separate substations
4.2 Transformers: 2 x 3000 kVA, 33kV/415V, ONAN cooling, Dyn11 vector group
4.3 Switchgear: Vacuum circuit breakers, SF6-free preferred
4.4 Protection: Differential protection, overcurrent and earth fault relays

5. CABLE SPECIFICATIONS
5.1 HV cables: XLPE insulated, armoured, 33kV grade
5.2 LV cables: Minimum 1100V grade, LSZH sheath for indoor runs
5.3 Cable trays: Galvanised steel, minimum 150mm wide, 50% fill factor

6. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
6.1 All electrical works per IS 1646, IS 3043, IE Rules 1956
6.2 Uptime Institute Tier III minimum - no single point of failure
6.3 Third party commissioning verification mandatory
6.4 CEIG approval required before energisation
