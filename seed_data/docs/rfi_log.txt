RFI-001
Subject: UPS room ventilation requirements
Raised by: MEP Contractor
Date: 15 Jan 2026
Question: Specification section 2 requires double conversion UPS but does not 
specify ventilation requirements for UPS battery rooms. What are the minimum 
air changes per hour required?
Response: Minimum 6 air changes per hour for VRLA battery rooms per 
IEEE 1187. Hydrogen detection required. Response issued 20 Jan 2026.
Status: CLOSED

RFI-002  
Subject: Cable tray fill factor clarification
Raised by: Electrical Contractor
Date: 28 Jan 2026
Question: Spec section 5.3 states 50% fill factor for cable trays. Does this 
apply to both power and data cable trays or power trays only?
Response: 50% fill applies to power cable trays only. Data cable trays 
to follow TIA-569 recommendations (40% fill). Response issued 2 Feb 2026.
Status: CLOSED

RFI-003
Subject: Chiller refrigerant substitution
Raised by: Mechanical Contractor  
Date: 5 Feb 2026
Question: R134a specified in MECH-SPEC-002 section 2.4. Can R1234yf be 
substituted given lower GWP and upcoming F-gas regulations?
Response: R1234yf is acceptable subject to submission of revised 
safety data sheet and confirmation of chiller manufacturer approval. 
Schneider chillers confirmed compatible. Response issued 12 Feb 2026.
Status: CLOSED

RFI-004
Subject: Generator paralleling requirements
Raised by: Electrical Contractor
Date: 18 Mar 2026
Question: Three generators specified with automatic synchronisation panel. 
Should all three generators be capable of paralleling simultaneously or 
is 2+1 standby configuration acceptable?
Response: All three must be capable of parallel operation simultaneously 
for maintenance scenarios. Revise synchronisation panel specification accordingly.
Status: OPEN - awaiting contractor revised proposal

RFI-005
Subject: Raised floor load rating
Raised by: IT Infrastructure Team
