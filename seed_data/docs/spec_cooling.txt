MECHANICAL & COOLING SPECIFICATION - DATA CENTRE BLOCK A
Project: Hyperscale Data Centre - Chennai Campus  
Document No: MECH-SPEC-002 Rev 1
Issued: February 2026

1. COOLING SYSTEM OVERVIEW
The data centre cooling system shall maintain IT space conditions of 
18-27 degrees Celsius inlet temperature per ASHRAE A1 class requirements.
Design PUE target: 1.4 or below.

2. CHILLED WATER PLANT
2.1 Chillers: 4 x 1500 kW centrifugal chillers (N+1 with 1 standby)
2.2 Chilled water supply: 7 degrees Celsius supply, 13 degrees return
2.3 COP: Minimum 6.0 at design conditions
2.4 Refrigerant: R134a or low-GWP alternative (R513A acceptable)
2.5 Accepted makes: Carrier, Trane, York, Daikin Applied

3. COMPUTER ROOM AIR HANDLERS (CRAH)
3.1 Type: Chilled water CRAH units, variable speed fans
3.2 Capacity: 60 kW per unit, 2N redundancy per aisle
3.3 Airflow: Hot aisle/cold aisle containment mandatory
3.4 Controls: BACnet integration with DCIM platform
3.5 Accepted makes: Stulz, Schneider, Vertiv Liebert

4. COOLING TOWERS
4.1 Type: Induced draft crossflow, fibre reinforced plastic
4.2 Capacity: Sized for full IT load plus 20% margin
4.3 Basin heaters: Required for ambient below 5 degrees
4.4 Water treatment: Chemical dosing system mandatory
4.5 Drift eliminators: Maximum 0.001% drift loss

5. PIPEWORK
5.1 Chilled water pipework: Black steel, schedule 40 minimum
5.2 Insulation: Closed cell foam, minimum 25mm thickness
5.3 Pipe supports: Clevis hangers at maximum 2.5m spacing
5.4 Leak detection: Linear leak detection cable under all raised floor areas

6. COMMISSIONING REQUIREMENTS
6.1 Witness testing of all chillers at factory before delivery
6.2 Integrated systems test (IST) for minimum 72 hours
6.3 All CRAH units to be commissioned with airflow measurement
6.4 Thermal mapping of IT space required post-commissioning
